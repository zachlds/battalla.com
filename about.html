Our Story

Battalla TCG was created by Michael and Aaron, two passionate game designers who wanted to bring a fresh strategic experience to the world of trading card games. With a focus on hero-based gameplay, tactical movement, and thematic depth, Battalla offers players a unique battlefield experience.

Our Mission

We believe in building a community where creativity, competition, and camaraderie thrive. Whether you're a casual player or a tournament veteran, Battalla is designed to challenge your mind and spark your imagination.
