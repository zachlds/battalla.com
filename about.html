A Note From The Creators

Battalla Creators

Welcome to Battalla! We're Michael and Aaron, lifelong friends and the creators of this exciting new card game based right here in Huntsville, AL.

Our journey with Battalla began from a simple desire: to create a unique and engaging card game that we could enjoy with our friends. Drawing inspiration from our love of strategy games and tabletop gaming, we embarked on a quest to bring our vision to life.

As avid fans of trading card games ourselves, we wanted to contribute something special to the TCG community. Battalla is the result of countless hours of brainstorming, playtesting, and creative collaboration, fueled by our passion for gaming and our dedication to crafting a memorable experience for players of all levels.

At its core, Battalla is about bringing people together, fostering camaraderie, and creating unforgettable memories. We believe in the power of games to connect people from all walks of life and provide a platform for shared experiences and friendly competition.

We're thrilled to share Battalla with you and invite you to join us on this epic adventure. Whether you're a seasoned TCG veteran or new to the world of card games, there's a place for you in the Battalla community. Together, let's embark on a journey of strategy, friendship, and endless fun!

Thank you for being a part of our story.

Sincerely,

Michael and Aaron
