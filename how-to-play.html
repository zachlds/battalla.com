Game Setup

Each player selects a hero and builds a deck. Place your hero on the battlefield and draw your opening hand.

Turn Phases

  1. Draw Phase
  2. Movement Phase
  3. Combat Phase
  4. End Phase

Keywords

Cards may include keywords like Charge, Guard, or Empower. Refer to the rulebook for full definitions.
