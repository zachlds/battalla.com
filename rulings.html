Rulings and Erratas

We make mistakes, this page is to help clear up rulings and fix typos or unintended card interactions.

If you have found a typo or have ruling questions, please contact us on one of our social platforms!

Last updated 8/27/24

Osiris

Typo:

HA: 1/Turn, you may discard 2 cards to revive a unit with a cost or 2 stamina or less (You cannot summon a unit discarded with this effect).

Should Read:

HA: 1/Turn, you may discard 2 cards to revive a unit with a cost of 2 stamina or less (You cannot summon a unit discarded with this effect).

Isis

Typo:

1/Turn: (3) Sacrifice an Egyptian unit to search your deck an Egyptian/ Assist and add it to your hand

Should Read:

1/Turn: (3) Sacrifice an Egyptian unit to search your deck for an Egyptian/ Assist and add it to your hand

Taizen Fukunsai

Typo:

Name is misspelt as "Taizen Fukansai" This is corrected in future prints

Tamamo no Mae

Typo:

"...when this unit leaves the field...."

Should Read:

"...when this hero leaves the field...."

Neglected Items

Typo:

Summon any number of Yokai/ Item cards attached to a unit (ignoring lane restrictions...

Should Read:

Summon any number of Yokai/ Item/ Unit cards attached to a unit (ignoring lane restrictions...

Tengu Shogi Game

Typo:

Move a Yokai unit or hero you control to an unoccupied lane...

Should Read:

Move a Yokai /Unit or Yokai/ Hero you control to an unoccupied lane...

Paper Shikigami

Rules Clarification:

When an assist card is played, it is not on the field and is not placed in the discard until the chain resolves (we consider the card "Floating" until then.) This means Paper Shikigami can NOT negate an assist that is being played from the hand.
